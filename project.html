My Projects

- Piring Nusantara

Piring Nusantara is an interactive Augmented Reality (AR) application that allows users to explore the rich culinary heritage of Indonesia through 3D models of traditional dishes from various regions. Developed using Unity, the app leverages AR technology to deliver an immersive and educational experience. With Piring Nusantara, users can point their devices at specific markers, and detailed 3D models of iconic Indonesian dishes such as rendang, sate, or nasi tumpeng will appear in real-time. Each model is accompanied by information on the dish’s origin, ingredients, and preparation methods, providing a deeper understanding of Indonesia’s culinary heritage.

- Puff & Poof

Puff & Poof is a dedicated online shop application for plush toy lovers, offering a wide variety of adorable and high-quality stuffed animals. Developed using Android Studio with Kotlin, this app provides a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience, allowing users to browse, select, and purchase their favorite plush toys with ease. Users can explore product details, view multiple images, and read customer reviews before making a purchase. The secure checkout process guarantees a safe and hassle-free transaction.

- Bonk Garage

Bonk Garage is an online shop app designed for diecast model car enthusiasts. Developed using Android Studio with Kotlin, the app offers a streamlined platform for browsing and purchasing a wide range of diecast model cars, from classic cars to modern supercars, catering to collectors of all ages. The app features an elegant and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to explore the various collections and find their favorite diecast models. - Pure Plate

Pure Plate is an online shop application designed for health-conscious individuals seeking nutritious and balanced meals. Developed using Android Studio with Kotlin, the app provides users with a curated selection of healthy meals, each accompanied by detailed nutritional information to help them make informed food choices. Pure Plate offers a smooth and intuitive shopping experience, where users can browse through various meal options, view ingredients, and analyze nutritional values such as calories, protein, fat, and carbohydrate content. Whether you're on a specific diet or just looking for wholesome meals, Pure Plate ensures you find the perfect dish for your lifestyle. The app’s secure payment and fast checkout process ensure a seamless user experience.

- Dok Dok

Dok Dok is a telemedicine app design to make healthcare services more accessible and affordable for underserved communities. By leveraging technology, Dok Dok connects patients with qualified healthcare professionals, providing medical consultations and advice remotely. This app is especially beneficial for individuals facing challenges in accessing healthcare due to geographical barriers or financial constraints. Developed with user convenience in mind, Dok Dok enables users to schedule virtual appointments, chat with doctors, and receive medical advice from the comfort of their homes. The app offers a range of healthcare services at a fraction of the cost of traditional medical consultations, helping users save on both time and money while still receiving quality care.
